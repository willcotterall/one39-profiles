The Story

“I've always believed the local church is where lives truly change — my heart is to build teams, disciple people, and help create a place where people can encounter God.”

Juan Daza's ministry calling began at thirteen years old during a transformative church retreat where he first sensed God speaking directly to him. Growing up in a Christian home with a father who entered ministry after seminary, Juan witnessed firsthand what it meant to serve God with your life. Though his early years included seasons of both faith and struggle, a serious accident in 2015 became his turning point, refocusing his heart and renewing his commitment to follow Christ.

Moving to Florida, Juan joined Lifepointe Church and dove into youth ministry leadership with remarkable results. Over four years as Youth Pastor, he tripled the youth volunteer team from ten to thirty people and grew attendance from thirty to nearly one hundred students. His investment in developing young leaders proved lasting — twenty students became active church volunteers, with four now serving in full-time ministry at the church today. Juan built sustainable systems for volunteer recruitment, creative coordination, and leadership development that continue to benefit the ministry.

In 2023, Juan embraced a pioneering challenge, helping plant Futuro Church in El Paso, Texas alongside his brother. As Assistant Pastor, he helped scale the church from a forty-person house church gathering to over 150 regular attendees. While simultaneously working as a Bible teacher to support his family, Juan built volunteer infrastructure and established ministry systems from the ground up. Now ready for his next chapter, Juan brings proven experience in team building, leadership development, and church growth, combined with a deep conviction that the local church is where lives truly change.

Five Voices Profile

Nurturer

Nurturers are champions of people and relational harmony. They are the advocates for others, often representing the voice of the unheard. Nurturers bring teams together and create environments where everyone feels valued and cared for. They build trust through genuine relationships and often respond to challenges with 'How is everyone feeling about this?'

Connector

Champion of relationships and strategic partnerships. Connectors rally people to causes and things they believe in. Connectors believe in a world where everyone can play and get excited about future opportunities and they want to make it happen! Connectors often respond to challenges with 'Whatever we need, I can get it, or I have a source.'
